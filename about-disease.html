Eye Disease Encyclopedia

Learn about common eye conditions and how they affect vision

Glaucoma

A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often caused by abnormally high pressure in the eye.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes complication affecting eyes, caused by damage to blood vessels in the retina.

Cataracts

A clouding of the eye's natural lens that affects vision, commonly related to aging.

Age-Related Macular Degeneration

A condition that affects the macula, the part of the eye that controls sharp, straight-ahead vision.

Dry Eye Syndrome

A condition where eyes don't produce enough tears or tears evaporate too quickly.

Retinal Detachment

A serious condition where the retina pulls away from the supportive tissue at the back of the eye.

Advancements in AI-Driven Eye Disease Detection

Early 2000s

First computer-aided diagnosis systems for retinal images with basic image processing techniques.

2012

Introduction of deep learning methods for medical image analysis, achieving modest accuracy for diabetic retinopathy detection.

2016

Google's DeepMind achieves expert-level accuracy for detecting diabetic retinopathy from retinal photographs.

2018

FDA approves first AI-based diagnostic system for autonomous detection of diabetic retinopathy.

2020

Multi-disease detection models emerge, capable of identifying multiple eye conditions from a single scan.

2023

Real-time mobile-based AI diagnostics become available, democratizing access to eye disease screening worldwide.
